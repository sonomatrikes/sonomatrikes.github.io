Joe RoTOTa Trail Ride Last Sunday of Every Month

Come join us on for an informal casual paced ride along the Joe Rodota trail from Santa Rosa, through Sebastopol to Forestville and back.

TOT stands for Trike Optional Tour, but all modes of human powered vehicles are welcome. If you happen to have a recumbent trike, maybe bring it along as it would be fun to have a big trike squadron touring down the bike path.

We start promptly at 10am at the Goodwill store parking lot off Stony Point Rd. and Hwy 12 adjacent to the Joe Rodota trail. There are several food options in Forestville for a bite to eat at our turn around point. We'll typically stop for coffee or ice cream on the trip, so you will return home calorie neutral.
