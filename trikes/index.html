Our Trikes

There are a wide range of recumbent trike styles available, with different ride heights, options, front suspension, rear suspension, folding for easy storage/travel/transport, or even fully weatherize velomobiles (enclosed trikes). We can help you find your way through all the options, and keep you within a desired budget, or build an amazing dream machine ready anything.

If you would like to know more, please contact us to setup a personalized demo appointment around your schedule, anytime from 7am-7pm, 7 days a week (depending of our family schedule :) A demo experience will usually take a hour or so, and if you want to explore any particular models further, we have day or week long rental options available.
